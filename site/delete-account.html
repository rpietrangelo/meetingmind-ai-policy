Delete Your Account

You can delete your MeetingMind account and all associated data at any time.

1

Open MeetingMind on your device

Make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.

2

Go to Settings

Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.

3

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the Privacy & Data section and tap Delete Account.

4

Confirm deletion

Follow the on-screen prompts to permanently delete your account.

⚠️ This action is permanent

  • All your meetings, transcripts, and notes will be permanently deleted
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion
  • Data is removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Cancel your Pro subscription before deleting to avoid future charges

Can't access the app?

If you no longer have access to the app and need your account deleted, email us at privacy@meetingmind-ai.com from the address associated with your account and we will delete it within 5 business days.
