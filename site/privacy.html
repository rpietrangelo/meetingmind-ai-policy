Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

The short version: MeetingMind processes all audio entirely on your device. Your voice and audio recordings never leave your phone. Only text outputs — transcripts, notes, and questions — are optionally synced to your account. You are in full control.

👤 Who We Are

MeetingMind ("we", "our", or "us") is an Android application developed by Rocco Pietrangelo. You can reach us at privacy@meetingmind-ai.com.

Our website is meetingmind-ai.com.

🎙️ Audio & On-Device Processing

MeetingMind uses your device's microphone to transcribe meetings. Here is exactly what happens with your audio:

  • Audio is processed entirely on your device using Whisper (an open-source speech recognition model) and Phi-3 Mini (an on-device AI model).
  • Audio files are never uploaded to any server, cloud, or third party — ever.
  • Audio is not stored on your device after processing. Only the resulting text (transcript) is saved.
  • Microphone access is used solely for meeting transcription and only while you are actively recording.

📋 What Data We Collect

Account Data (required to use the app)

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Account creation date
  • Subscription tier (Free or Pro)

Meeting Data (only if cloud sync is enabled)

  • Meeting title, date, and duration
  • Text transcript of your meeting
  • AI-generated key notes and open questions
  • Speaker labels (if speaker detection is used)

If you disable cloud sync in Settings, all meeting data stays exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Usage Data

  • Number of meetings recorded this month (for free tier enforcement)
  • App crash reports (anonymous, via Firebase Crashlytics if enabled)

🚫 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Audio recordings — never uploaded, never stored on our servers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Device identifiers beyond what Firebase Auth requires
  • Browsing history or data from other apps

☁️ How We Store Your Data

MeetingMind uses Google Firebase for authentication and optional cloud storage:

  • Firebase Authentication — stores your email and account credentials securely.
  • Firestore — stores your meeting data when sync is enabled. Data is stored in Google's us-central1 region.
  • Firebase Storage — stores archived transcripts (text only) for meetings older than 90 days (Pro accounts).

All data in Firebase is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

🔒 Data Retention

  • Free accounts: Meeting data is accessible for 30 days. Older meetings are hidden but not deleted unless you choose to delete them.
  • Pro accounts: Meeting data is accessible for 1 year. Transcripts older than 90 days are archived to long-term storage.
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all your data — including all meetings, transcripts, and account information — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

🤝 Third Parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and cloud storage. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • SendGrid (Twilio) — used to deliver email meeting reports when you request them. Only your email address and meeting content are transmitted. SendGrid Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — handles all payment processing for Pro subscriptions. We do not store your payment details. Google Payments Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

MeetingMind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

🌍 Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account, or by contacting us.
  • Portability: Export all your meeting data via Settings → Export All My Data.
  • Correction: Update your account information at any time in the app.
  • Opt-out of sync: Disable cloud sync at any time in Settings — your data will remain on-device only.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of MeetingMind after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
